About Our Group

The Soft Computing and Machine Learning (SCML) group is a WhatsApp based group created in 2019 with the objective of encouraging scientific and technical research related discussions among its group members. The subjects of discussion mainly include Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Engineering Optimization. At present we have members from different IITs/NITs/IIITs/Indian central government and state government engineering institutes/ Indian private engineering institutes/ European technical institutes/ American technical institutes/ and from IT industries as well. Initiators of this group: Dr. Sandipan Dhar (Post Doc. @IIT Bombay, India) and Dr. Arijit Nandi (Advanced ML Researcher @Eurecat, Barcelona, Spain).
