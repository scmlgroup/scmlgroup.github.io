About Us

Our Mission

The Soft Computing and Machine Learning Group (SCML) is dedicated to advancing research in artificial intelligence, computational intelligence, and machine learning. Our interdisciplinary team collaborates on cutting-edge projects that push the boundaries of what's possible in AI.

Research Focus Areas

Deep Learning & Neural Networks

Developing novel architectures and training methods for deep neural networks, including transformers, CNNs, and RNNs.

Natural Language Processing

Advancing language understanding, generation, and translation through state-of-the-art NLP techniques.

Computer Vision

Exploring visual understanding, object detection, and image analysis using deep learning approaches.

Federated Learning

Developing privacy-preserving machine learning techniques for distributed data environments.

Explainable AI

Making machine learning systems transparent and interpretable for real-world applications.

AI Ethics & Fairness

Ensuring responsible development and deployment of AI systems with ethical considerations.

Our History

2018

Group Formation

SCML Group was established with a focus on soft computing methodologies.

2020

Expansion to Deep Learning

Expanded research scope to include deep learning and neural networks.

2022

Industry Partnerships

Established collaborations with leading tech companies and research institutions.

2024

Current Focus

Leading research in transformer architectures, federated learning, and AI ethics.
