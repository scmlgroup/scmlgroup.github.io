About This Event

Join us for an intensive hands-on workshop on machine learning and deep learning. This workshop is designed for researchers, students, and professionals who want to gain practical experience with state-of-the-art machine learning techniques.

Participants will work with real datasets, implement neural networks from scratch, and explore advanced topics such as convolutional neural networks (CNNs), recurrent neural networks (RNNs), and transformer architectures.
